The Honor Roll - Rebecca Eaton named to TIME Magazine's 100 Most Influential People

  • 1 month ago
  • Like
  • Share
  • Email
  • by Admin

There are plenty of opportunities to stay healthy throughout the season in Crystal City. Outdoor Yoga takes place every Monday morning at 7:00 AM in the Crystal City Water Park, across the street from 1750 Crystal Drive. The Crystal City FRESHFARMS Farmers Market is on Crystal Drive between 18th and 20th from 3:00 PM to 7:00 PM every Tuesday. Outdoor Zumba begins its second summer on Wednesday, May 11, at noon in the courtyard in front of 2121 Crystal Drive. A free class, Zumba fuses hypnotic Latin rhythms and easy-to-follow moves to create a one-of-a-kind fitness program that will blow you away. Finally, Street Hockey takes place every Thursday at 6:30 p.m. A new athletic program in Crystal City, Crystal Rolls - Street Hockey, in partnership with the DC Social Sports Club (DCSSC), launched May 5. Street Hockey enthusiasts can join as individuals ($25) or form a team ($250) for 11 weeks of Thursday evening matches in the parking lot adjacent to 220 Twentieth Street. Click here to register.
