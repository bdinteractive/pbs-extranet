Tools, Data, and Text to Document Public Television's Effectiveness

The resources on this page provide research data, sample text, graphics, and multimedia resources for stations to use in advancing information about the American public's support for public broadcasting. Looking exclusively for results from our annual trust survey? Visit http://mypbs.org/trust/.

This page also includes key elements from PBS' microsite, valuePBS.org, which presents facts and information that deliver on our call to action: Trusted, Valued, and Essential. Stations, supporters, the public and other stakeholders can call upon valuePBS for evidence of our continuing value and importance to the American people.
